Prize Money

Members of the winning team will split the prize money equally among its members.

First prize: $6,000

Second prize: $4,000

Third prize: $2,000

Winners can use the prize money to further develop their proposed solution.

They may also receive additional support from the college for further project development.

ESJ is an annual competition striving to better our communities with innovative social justice solutions. To learn more, go to our About page!
